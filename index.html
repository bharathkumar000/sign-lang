About SignSpeak AI

Camera Detection

Uses your device's camera with MediaPipe hand tracking to detect 21 hand landmarks in real-time at up to 30 FPS.

ASL Recognition

Recognizes all 26 letters of the ASL finger-spelling alphabet using geometric analysis of finger positions and angles.

Text & Audio

Converts detected signs to text in real-time. Uses the Web Speech API to speak the text aloud with adjustable speed.

Runs Locally

100% client-side processing. No data is sent to any server. Your camera feed stays private on your device.
